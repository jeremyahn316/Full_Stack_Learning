The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time

Frozen

My first Disney princess crush, musical was perfect, and Olaf was the comedy gag that I never knew I needed but got.

Tangled

The original Disney/Pixar that I fell in love with. The musical was also amazing and especially love the duo the main characters sing at the end.

Your Name

My first anime film ever, biggest plot twist and loved the dynamic between the main girl and guy. The concept felt fresh and original.
